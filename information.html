Men's Salon by André D'Avis

Bonjour! Welcome to André D'Avis, DFW's premier contemporary male salon.

Allow us to provide your next exceptional hair and body services in a relaxing, masculine environment.

ABOUT

From the beginning, André D'Avis opened its doors on the principle that every man deserves to feel the confidence of looking his best, a goal that can only be achieved by tailoring our highly specialized services for each patron.

When you step into the salon, you will be given the attention you deserve. Highly experienced specialists provide an initial consultation to determine the best style and service treatments for you as an individual. André D'Avis prides itself on staying current with men's styling trends and grooming products. As such, the specialists don't just provide haircuts, they provide a deliberate approach to solve problems and give great direction for the modern man to best express his nature and personality.

André D'Avis provides scissor-only haircuts to achieve the most complimentary service possible. The cuts are softer, more natural, and grow out beautifully, staying in harmony with the natural rhythm and texture of the hair.

This level of care applies to all services André D'Avis provide, giving you, our patron, an unbeatable experience. After your services are complete, you'll depart with an assortment of complementary products specially selected to help you maintain your new look for weeks to come.

TEAM

André D'Avis believes ongoing education and exploration are key to keeping up with current trends. Every specialist, from head stylist to floor assistant, is provided stipend funds and time away from the salon to this end - because investing in the team is an investment for our guests.

André D'Avis

André D'Avis, owner and stylist, is an accomplished professional. He was top of his high school class and graduated early before immediately enrolling in Avery Star Institute, the top beauty school in New York City. After graduating, D'Avis continued his training at the leading men's hair salons in uptown Manhattan, where he saw regular clients including celebrities, leaders, and artists. Today, he offers his services to men of all occupations and backgrounds, with all hair types.

Erin Brunson

Erin Brunson, co-founder and colorist, graduated from Dulin Cosmetology Institute in Dallas and has a cumulative 15 years of experience working in lavish salons in New York City, where her passion flourished. Now, she has returned to her home state to provide the best experience found in DFW. Her expertise includes all backgrounds and hair types.

Eleanor Price

Eleanor "Ela" Price, salon coordinator, stylist, and colorist, had 6 years of experience before training under D'Avis. Now, 3 years later, her passion and skillset have grown and she has a proven track record of quality work. Her strengths include 1:1 consultations to get patrons looking their best.

Christina Harrison

Christina Harrison, stylist and colorist, grew up in Austin where her grandmother owned a salon. Christina took an interest from an early age and trained under her guidance. After graduating high school, she enrolled at Dulin Cosmetology Institute where she excelled. Now her focus is on hair styling, as well as skin and nail care.

Joanna Key

Joanna Key, stylist and colorist, has 9 months of education at Saylor Cosmetology in Chicago and 12 years of trained experience. Her exceeding talent has opened many doors for her, including an opportunity to work overseas for 2 years. Today, she enjoys not only keeping with the current trends but going above and beyond to create new ones.
